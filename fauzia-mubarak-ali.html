Who Is Fauzia Mubarak Ali?

Fauzia Mubarak Ali is a human rights activist and the wife of Cat Stevens, a famous British singer-songwriter and humanitarian.

She was born on March 24, 1958 in Karachi, Pakistan and graduated with a Bachelor’s degree in Urdu Literature from Kinnaird College for Women and a Master’s degree in Comparative Literature from the University of Columbia. 

She is the founder of the Human Rights Advocates Association (HRAA), which works to promote social justice in Pakistan and the Middle East.
