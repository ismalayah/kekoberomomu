The racing community of NASCAR mourns as it recently lost an indispensable member of its production team, Mike Thompson.

Thompson’s journey with Fox Sports began in 2003, and he moved to the NASCAR production team in 2005. His talent and creativity in storytelling garnered him recognition and respect from colleagues and viewers alike.
